About us

Our Company

At CCBnb, we believe that travel is not just about visiting places; it's about creating meaningful connections and experiencing the world through the lens of local hospitality. Founded by a dynamic team of Centennial College students – Yuzhi Cheng, Mehmet Kurt Ali, and Juan Moscoso Lagos – CCBnb is more than just a platform; it's a community-driven endeavor to redefine the way you travel.

Our Team

Yuzhi Cheng

With a passion for creating seamless user experiences, Yuzhi ensures that CCBnb is not just a website but a doorway to unforgettable journeys. His dedication to innovation and user-centric design shapes every aspect of the CCBnb platform.

Mehmet Kurt Ali

Mehmet brings a global perspective to CCBnb, infusing the platform with diversity and inclusivity. His commitment to fostering a sense of belonging for travelers from all walks of life is reflected in the unique offerings and personalized experiences available through CCBnb.

Juan Moscoso Lagos

Juan's entrepreneurial spirit and business acumen drive the growth and sustainability of CCBnb. His strategic vision ensures that CCBnb is not only a traveler's delight but also a valuable resource for homeowners seeking to share their spaces and stories.
